UPVC

UPVC is the third largest plastic material in the world, and it has great flexibility that makes it one of the most important window and door frames. It is characterized by insulation of rain, sound and wind. It also provides protection against insects and provides salt water resistance. It is available in a wide variety of colors and does not require large production capacity with recyclability.
